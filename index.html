Composed over 4 years full of changes and chance meetings, the third self-produced Panzer Kunst album finally emerges from the shadows.
Panzer's hip-hoptronica is made up of multicolored patterns: icy melodies and pulpy rhythms meet acoustic instruments, the hyperactive scratches of DJ Odilon and the serbian phonetics of Blokovski, Etik & Souldee.
With a final coat of polish thanks to Christopher Leary's mastering and Emi Sakurai's modernist design, Dazzle Camouflage sets sail plotting a course that leaves it floating outside of today's overly hyped electronic currents.

The album is available on BandCamp in 2 packages:
